A wildfire is an uncontrolled fire in an area of combustible vegetation.

Wildfires can:

Happen anytime and anywhere especially in forest area

Happen anytime provided that fuel,oxygen and heat(the 3 parameters for a fire) are present

Sometimes, fires occur naturally, ignited by heat from the sun or a lightning strike.On a hot summer's day when drought conditions peak, something as small as a spark from a train wheel can ignite a raging wildfire.
